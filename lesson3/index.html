Site Name:

Otavalo Valley Chamber

Site Purpose:

The Chamber of the Otavalo Valley, as a responsible local organization, creates commercial links by associating local and regional commerce with businesses and professionals from the community to develop marketing strategies, such as the purchase and sale of products and services between associates and the establishment of real prices for keep trade orderly.
Through our service, you can receive:

  • Access to networking events or community events. This helps you connect with more members of the community and gives your business positive press.
  • Assistance with marketing, accounting or security. This can be a more affordable option compared to hiring or outsourcing those things.
  • Access to various educational resources such as workshops, seminars and other learning opportunities. Learning more about business can help you grow your business.
  • Advice on tax, labor, commercial, public contracting, consumer protection, intellectual property, etc.
  • We have various virtual platforms, as well as an associated municipal radio station, so you can expand your contacts and help promote your products and/or services.
