Target Audience:

Our target audience is all entrepreneurs, owners of their own businesses or people looking to formalize their business, merchants, professionals who provide their services and artisan, textile, agricultural producers, and more types of producers, since they need sustainable support within a broader trade and market network to grow within the regional market or integrate into it in the best way through access to strategies specifically designed to favor and strengthen the commercial and market sector in the local area of the Otavalo Valley and its nearby regions.
