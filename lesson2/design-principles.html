White Space

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

The site has adequate white space which helps to differentiate between one element and another even in a small window. The white space used on this site also provides a sense of organization and helps create a simple interface for easy user navigation.

PARC: Alignment

Avianca

avianca.com

The alignment used in the buttons creates a proper connection between elements that provides a sense of harmony and makes accessing the different functionalities of the site easy. The alignment used on this site improves readability even when there are multiple buttons in a reduced viewport. Proper alignment makes the design look clean.

PARC: Contrast

Adidas

adidas.com

On this site the notable contrast used between foreground and background colors improves readability and provides a visual hierarchy of fonts, images, and content. Also, the contrast used on this site suits the purpose of the site and avoids hard-to-read text.
